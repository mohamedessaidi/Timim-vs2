Our Story

Located in the vibrant surf town of Tamraght, Boho-Chic celebrates the fusion of Moroccan tradition and coastal lifestyle. We source organic, local ingredients from nearby farms and fishermen, honoring the rich culinary heritage while embracing the relaxed vibe of surf culture. Our dishes tell a story of the land, sea, and sun-drenched terraces that make this place magical.
